# Amazon-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard
1. Project Title / Headline

Amazon Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI Interactive Report
A data-driven Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize Amazon’s product performance—offering insights into sales trends, product categories, and customer engagement metrics.

2. Short Description / Purpose

The Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key business metrics such as Year-to-Date (YTD) and Quarter-to-Date (QTD) sales, product performance, and customer reviews. It helps business teams and stakeholders track revenue trends, identify top-performing products, and make data-informed strategic decisions.

3. Tech Stack

This dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
• Power BI Desktop – For designing, visualizing, and publishing reports.
• Power Query Editor – For data transformation, cleaning, and combining multiple data sources.
• DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used to calculate YTD, QTD, % contribution, and dynamic measures.
• Data Modeling – Established relationships between product, category, and sales data tables for interactive filtering.
• File Format – .pbix for report development and .png for presentation/portfolio sharing.

4. Data Source
Source: Amazon product sales dataset (sample e-commerce data).
It includes structured information on:
Product Categories (e.g., Men Shoes, Men Clothes)
Monthly & Weekly Sales,Product Reviews & Ratings,Quantity Sold (YTD & QTD)
Data was preprocessed in Power Query for standardization and aggregated by category and time period.

5. Features / Highlights
• Business Problem

Amazon sellers and analysts require a consolidated view of product performance across different categories and time periods. Without a unified visualization, identifying sales patterns, customer engagement, and product demand becomes challenging.

• Goal of the Dashboard

To create an interactive sales performance dashboard that enables users to:

Track total sales and product demand (YTD & QTD).

Identify top-performing categories and products.

Analyze weekly and monthly sales trends.

Compare product reviews and engagement levels.

Support strategic sales planning and marketing optimization.

• Walkthrough of Key Visuals

Top KPIs (Top Row): $1.30M YTD Sales,$461.79K QTD Sales,16.28K Products Sold (YTD),9M Reviews (YTD)
These KPIs give a quick overview of business performance.

Sales by Month (Line Chart):
Displays sales trends throughout the year, highlighting peak and low-performing months for sales forecasting.

Sales by Week (Column Chart):
Shows weekly sales distribution, helping identify specific periods of high customer activity.

Sales by Product Category (Table):
Breaks down YTD and QTD sales by product category (e.g., Men Shoes, Men Clothes) with percentage contribution to total sales.

Top 5 Products by YTD Sales (Bar Chart):
Highlights the products generating the most revenue, useful for inventory and promotion decisions.

Top 5 Products by YTD Reviews (Bar Chart):
Displays products with the most customer engagement, linking performance to customer satisfaction.

Interactive Filters (Left Panel):
Allows dynamic filtering by Product Category and Quarter (Qtr), enabling customized analysis views.

• Business Impact & Insights

Performance Tracking: Simplifies monitoring of sales and customer metrics in real time.

Category Insights: Reveals that Men Shoes dominate revenue (72.44% of YTD Sales).

Strategic Planning: Enables businesses to focus marketing on high-performing categories and optimize low-performing ones.
